🔴 Emergency · Critical Response

GBP Hijack
Recovery

When a Google Business Profile is hijacked — whether through a malicious ownership transfer, fraudulent edit submission, or platform-level suppression — the operational consequences are immediate and severe. Incoming calls reroute. Address data corrupts. The phone stops ringing, and the business haemorrhages revenue in real time.

This is not a passive SEO problem. This is an active digital emergency requiring forensic identification, evidentiary documentation, and a precisely structured redressal submission to Google's Business Profile infrastructure.

Our Hijack Recovery mandate begins with a complete cross-referential audit: we compare your authoritative business records — website, registration documents, utility verification — against the corrupted listing data. Every discrepancy is logged, screenshotted, and timestamped. This evidence chain forms the submission package.

We then execute a targeted Google Business Redressal Complaint, constructed to meet Google's internal review criteria with the appropriate evidentiary weight. The majority of our reinstatements are confirmed within 24–72 hours of submission.

🟢 Core · Foundational

Entity
Verification

Reinstatement is the beginning, not the end. Once a Google Business Profile is restored — whether from suspension, hijacking, or a lapsed verification — the underlying structural vulnerabilities that enabled the incident remain if left unaddressed.

Entity Verification is our foundational hardening protocol. It ensures that your business is unambiguously, authoritatively, and consistently represented across every platform and data source that Google's infrastructure consults when evaluating listing legitimacy.

This encompasses your NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) across all directory citations, your Google Knowledge Panel entity status, schema markup accuracy on your web property, and the verifiability of your business documentation trail. A business that is easy for Google to verify is a business that is difficult for bad actors to displace.

The Entity Verification mandate produces a hardened, documented, multi-source entity footprint that functions as your digital notarisation — making future hijack attempts structurally more difficult to execute and easier to rebuff.

NAP Consistency Audit
Cross-platform Name, Address, Phone verification and remediation across all major directories and citation sources.
🔗
Schema Markup Validation
Structured data audit and implementation ensuring Google can parse and trust your entity data with precision.
🏛️
Knowledge Panel Verification
Google Knowledge Panel claim and entity verification where applicable, strengthening authoritative listing status.

A comprehensive entity health document delivered post-engagement, detailing every remediation made and its rationale.

🔵 Growth · Strategic

AI Search
Optimisation

The search paradigm has shifted. AI-driven language models — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude — are now functioning as de facto business directories. When a prospective client asks an AI assistant for a recommendation, the AI does not consult the same ranking signals that Google Search uses. It consults its training data, its retrieval-augmented sources, and the structured entity information it can access.

Businesses that are poorly represented in the data sources AI systems consult are effectively invisible to an increasingly large segment of buyers — even if they rank well in traditional search.

Our AI Search Optimisation mandate is a strategic protocol designed to make your business legible, trustworthy, and recommendable to AI systems. This involves structured entity optimisation, authoritative citation building, Wikipedia and Wikidata entity validation where applicable, and the construction of consistent, factual, structured content that AI systems can confidently surface in response to relevant queries.

This is not SEO as it was practised in 2019. This is the emerging discipline of AI discoverability engineering, and it demands a fundamentally different technical and editorial approach.

Trust
Monitoring

Digital presence is not a static asset. A Google Business Profile that is compliant and accurately represented today can be compromised tomorrow — through a fraudulent edit, a spam listing at your address, a review bombing campaign, or a platform-level algorithmic change that misclassifies your listing.

The Trust Monitoring retainer is a continuous, active surveillance protocol for your digital front door. We do not wait for you to notice something is wrong. We are watching so that you never have to.

Subscribers to the Trust Monitoring retainer receive monthly compliance crawls of their web properties — scanning for broken policy links, outdated compliance statements, and newly-introduced regulatory requirements. We also conduct regular GBP listing health checks, third-party citation monitoring, and AI visibility tracking.

When an anomaly is detected, it is remediated before it becomes an emergency. This is the operational posture of a business that treats its digital presence with the same seriousness it applies to its physical premises.
